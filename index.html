The Briar Poems

by Jim Wayne Miller

Miller's poems are among the most beloved in Appalachia. They're often read out loud when poets in the region congregate.

Kinfolks - The Wilgus Story

by Gurney Norman

Gnomon's best-selling title, over 22,000 copies sold. “This is a special book, sometimes hilarious, sometimes bittersweet.” — Library Journal

Payne Hollow

by Harlan Hubbard

A best-seller. “It joins that work of Henry Thoreau...and a growing shelf of wisdom literature for the alternative lifestyles movement.” Harlan and Anna Hubbard lived on the Ohio River where they started a life of sustenance farming before it became fashionable.
