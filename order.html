How to Order from Gnomon Press

  • Ordering direct from Gnomon via email (jgnomon@gmail.com) or phone (502-223-1858) benefits us the most and we keep a large inventory in stock.

  • We charge list price and individuals pay only $4.50 per order for shipping and handling.

  • We accept checks and money orders via mail.

  • Kentucky residents must add 6% sales tax.

  • For retail store and institution orders, college bookstore orders, and for author readings, please contact us directly for discount schedules.

  • We do not accept credit card orders.

About Us

Founded in 1965 by publisher and award-winning book designer Jonathan Greene, Gnomon Press specializes in books of high quality in the fields of literature and photography.

All books are printed on non-acid paper and designed to last for years.

Most of our titles are smythe-sewn.
